WELCOME TO THE EXPERIMENT

This is a task investigating how we process rapidly presented sentences.

You will be presented with pairs of SENTENCES. They will be presented very quickly, one after the other. Your task is to determine if the pair are identical or different from one another.

Any noticable change counts as DIFFERENT.

Press '2' if the pair are the SAME. Press '1' if they are DIFFERENT.

The experiment takes 20 more minutes to complete.

When you are ready, press SPACE to continue.
